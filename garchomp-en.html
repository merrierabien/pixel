Garchomp




I like Pokemon games. I had the occasion to play almost all the games, so it is a pleasure to draw things from this universe.
Here, I used a 3D model from the games X/Y to make this Garchomp. I drew over it with pixels. It was interesting and it was the occasion to learn, and understand how colors worked together.
