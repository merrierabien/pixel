Angry Impostor




Who never heard of Among Us ? This game developed by InnerSloth made an impression and was very popular. It was also a huge event for the Internet and specifically for the meme culture. A real icon, the little character of the Impostor made me laugh several times in many different situations. However, it's not the only meme that made me come to this result. There was also the Meme Man, a 3D model of a human head which is often used in absurd photomontages. There is just one of them in which he is seen getting angry, with the subtitle "angery". This is the spirit I wanted to recreate here.
This drawing is a pure Internet product, and I'm rather proud of it.
