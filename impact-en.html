Impact




What if blood fell from the sky ? An eye cries, but moreover, it suffers.

I indeed watched Neon Genesis Evangelion a few days before drawing this. Some will see an obvious reference. Yes, you are right !
The vastness of the universe around the Earth is not highlighted here. The thing I was focused on is the fate of the Earth, and what happens to it. A consequence of an event the Earthlings could have caused, for example. Your fate will always catch you, even if you thought you were free from it.
The tears and the end of the life are coming slowly on this drawing. The Impact is inevitable.
