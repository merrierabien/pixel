Red Rose




To create a bigger piece containing a rose, I had to practice in order to be able to draw one correctly. Here is one of them. I spent a long time looking at pictures of roses on the Internet to understand its shape.
When I drew this one, I had been surprised because I did not know I could do that. I reused the exact same rose later for the bigger piece.
