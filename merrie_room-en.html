Lain




It is one of my biggest drawings, 128x128 !
I represented this character, who is the same as the character on Profile picture 1, Profile picture 2 and Profile picture 3, laying on her bed. To give texture and not to have a plain drawing, I tried working on shadows and lights, mostly on her clothes. We can see several hues of gray and green so that she does not totally look like a 2D character.
On the walls, we can see many posters. One of them does not exist, but the others are all references to previous drawings that I have made. Did you see the flags ?
