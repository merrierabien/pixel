Spatial Creatures




The creatures' appearance is pure luck. I was looking for a shape that would inspire me, and this little creature appeared by dint of scribble on my canvas. Aren't they cute ?
After putting some of them in a sandbox, I wanted to put them in space, as an extraterrestrial population living on the Moon.
Thus, they are creatures from outer space who plays on their planet
There is a resemblance with the logo in Factory.
