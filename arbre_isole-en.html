Isolated Tree




I have made this drawing for Wafflaaar's wafflaaartuque organized to celebrate his 2 000 followers on Instagram. A color palette was imposed, a very cool one I used to draw a tree.
I think it is a pretty tree when I compare it with what I have done in the past. I like the way I arranged the colors to make some shadow on the tree. Working with a limited palette is always tough for me because I really need to think about which color is going to represent what, it's a tricky game sometimes. I juggle with dithering and simple shapes to make the drawing better.
