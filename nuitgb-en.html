Retro Night




It is the continuation of my work with the GameBoy color palette. I like this palette for the reason that it forces us to pay attention to the legibility of our drawing. We must think about what we want to show, what is necessary and what is not, so that the others can understand or recreate what we imagined.
Here, I represented a sky by night. It is something simple, but the organization of the colors is essential. I had to pay attention to avoid the situation where two adjacent elements are colored with the same color. In addition, the GameBoy palette is only composed of hues of the same color, here it is green. When the entire picture is dark or illuminated, I can end up juggling between the same colors and I can easily get mixed up. When I drew this, I was trying out, and it is this kind of approach that makes me learn.
