Candle




Animation, for me, it seems very complex. When I see people working on animations, it's complex. That's why I tried at my scale, with something very small, but not just a rectilinear uniform movement. That very small thing is this candle. I like staring at candles' flame. It is the only thought I had in mind when making this drawing.
The small particles escaping the main flame gives a lively effect to a movement which, in my opinion, lack of life. It's a first try, and it satisfies me for what it is. We really feel that the flame was the center of the attention, the other parts are not well-made.
