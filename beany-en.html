Iced tea




This drawing had been made for MasterBeany's beany9k organized to celebrate his 9 000 followers on Instagram. The color palette is very nice, and I remember all the fantastic other entries for the challenge. I struggled to find what to draw with the color palette. At the beginning, I thought about a sunset on the ocean, but the result did not satisfy me, so I restarted from scratch. The drink is well emphasized in my opinion, I'm thirsty now !
