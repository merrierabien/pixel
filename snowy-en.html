Snowy




I'm impressed by the isometric drawings in pixel art. Many beautiful things can come out of this style. That is why I decided to make some research about it and create one.
First things first, I had to learn how it works. It is simply a question of volumes, 3D figures, and I had never thought of doing it rigorously with exact lines. Once I got this, I started trying out.
I know nothing about perspective. The drawing contained some obvious inconsistencies. I asked a friend for help, and he told me what was wrong. Thanks to him, I was able to correct some problems. The result is cool for a first try, I'm proud of it.
