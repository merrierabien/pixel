Madeline




I am a huge fan of Celeste. The character of Madeline is very inspiring. There was a time when I refinished the game once per year !
Because I really love this indie game, I wanted to draw this drawing of Madeline, the main character of the game.
The first version of this drawing was plain, without any texture. I had to find a way to give it some life by adding light reflections and some particles.
At the end, I think she kind of looks like a strawberry, which suits with the game I guess.
