Profile picture 2




I wanted to make a better profile picture than the previous one with the new experience I had acquired. I took a less narrowed view, we can now see the top of her chest. Then, I drew her without any particular plan. It is still the same character, she just cut her hair a bit.
I like this character, but she still does not have a name. The background is made of random lines. I wanted something not symmetrical and not straight, I wanted a background more fantasist and natural.
She will come back, be sure of it !
