Starry City




I have always been impressed by the use of dithering to create color gradients. This is what I wanted to try here, we can see the sky going from a dark blue to warmer colors like brown or orange. However, the dithering patterns are not diversified at all. When we look at the drawing from a distance, the effect seems to look good.
The main idea is the starry night. I wanted the sky to dominate the city while looking nice and calming. The city in the foreground, in darkness, is not much emphasized nor detailed. The buildings are just some big black simple shapes so that the attention is centered on something else. The city is just a setting in order to let the beauty of the sky and the calm of the boat exist.
The boat represents calm and quietness. It sails on a motionless sea. Everything is under control, giving the drawing a reassuring atmosphere.
