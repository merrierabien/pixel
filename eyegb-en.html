Retro Eye




The eyes are a beautiful part of the face. We can often hear people saying "you have beautiful eyes" for example. They represent thus a captivating subject for me.
I kept on using a GameBoy color palette because I start to familiarize with it. I wanted to use more dithering to have extra hues to use more colors for the pupil's light.
I let some pixels at the border of the shapes as I saw the effect it could produce on others artworks. I think it brings a beneficial effect to the drawing, even if I cannot properly describe it.
