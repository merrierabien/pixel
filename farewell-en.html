Farewell




I wanted to do it a second time. I really like the video game Celeste, and more specifically, I love its original soundtrack composed by Lena Raine. It is an integral and essential part of the game, one of the main reasons why I love the game that much. Thus, I decided to draw the Farewell album's cover by Nina Matsumoto using the GameBoy color palette. It had been a long time since I last used this color palette, so I tried to mix these wishes to create something nice.
For the first time, I also used the original resolution of the console : 160x144. It brought new challenges concerning the screen space and the layout of the elements. I had some difficulties with Madeline's face for those reasons. Another issue was to organize the colors to keep the drawing understandable, especially between the hair and the arms of the character.
The cover has not been reproduced identical to the original. I revisited it, taking inspiration from the game and from my personal feelings.
