Picnic




At the beginning of May 2022, I made several small drawings with limited color palettes. This time, I wanted something bigger, more free. I imagined a scene, and it looked almost like the final result. I added the carpet at the last moment because I felt that it was empty next to the tree.
This drawing took me a whole week, by small sessions. Most of the ideas came progressively, adding themselves in the scene for a result that I like now.
